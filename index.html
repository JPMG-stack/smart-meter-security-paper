📋 Abstract

Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) relies on reliable data communication for efficient energy management and grid control. This paper extends the experimental work of Kumar et al. (2026) through computational simulation, expanding the scope to 12 smart meters from 5 manufacturers, 6 communication technologies, and 10 cyberattack types.

Key Contributions:
  • Statistically robust metrics with 95% confidence intervals (30 repetitions per scenario)
  • Random Forest classifier achieving 100% detection accuracy
  • Rule-based Intrusion Detection System (IDS)
  • Quantitative economic impact analysis for networks of 10,000 meters
