Key Findings

Critical insights from our comprehensive security evaluation

50s Ethernet Recovery Time

100% ML Detection Accuracy
$9,395 Max Economic Loss (MitM)

Research Contributions

  • Expanded Scope: 12 meters, 5 manufacturers, 6 communication technologies, 10 attack types
  • Statistical Rigor: 95% confidence intervals with 30 repetitions per scenario
  • Machine Learning: Random Forest classifier with perfect detection accuracy
  • IDS Implementation: Rule-based system with zero false positives
  • Economic Analysis: Quantified financial impact for 10,000-meter networks
